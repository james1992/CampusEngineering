Cooling Tower Free Chlorine Troubleshooting



For more information, please contact NALCO.


NOTE: Oxidizing Biocide (ST70) does not feed on Tues, Thurs, Sat, Sun. Free Chlorine level is expected to be low on these days. Non-Oxidizing Biocide feeds on Tues, Thurs.


    Below Range (< .5 ppm)

  • If it is Monday, Wednesday, or Friday, and the value is less than 0.5 ppm and the conductivity is in range, increase ST70 pump speed 10%.
    • If Free Chlorine continues to be low after 1 pump speed increase.
      • Check ST70 feed time vs. testing time, check inventory, check if pump is primed and has power, check injection line for a clog.
  • Contact Nalco if above 90% on speed.

    Above Range (> 2.0 ppm)

  • If it is M,W,F and the value is greater than 2.0 ppm and the conductivity is in range, reduce ST70 pump speed 10%.
  • If it is M,W,F and the value is greater than 2.0 ppm and the conductivity is below or above range, correct conductivity first and re-test Free Chlorine.
  • Contact Nalco if below 10% on speed.
