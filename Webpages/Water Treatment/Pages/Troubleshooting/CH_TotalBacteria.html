Chilled/Hot Water Total Bacteria Troubleshooting



For more information, please contact NALCO.


    Total Aerobic Bacteria

  • 10,000 cells/ml or below: No action required.
  • 10,001 – 100,000 cells/ml:
    • Inspect testing logs and ORP curves for the prior week.
    • Double the halogen feed for the next 3 feeds.
  • 100,001 – 999,999 cells/ml:
    • Inspect testing logs and ORP curves for the prior two weeks
    • Inspect the Cooling tower for visible signs of biological fouling conditions
    • Inspect biocide feed pumps.
    • Confirm the non‐oxidizing biocide feed.
    • Bring free halogen up to 2 PPM for 24 hours
  • 1 million+ cells/ml:
    • Inspect the Cooling tower for visible signs of biological fouling conditions and photograph conditions for Team review.
    • Inspect testing logs and ORP curves for the last month.
    • Inspect biocide feed pumps.
    • Confirm the non‐oxidizing biocide feed.
    • Institute Decontamination Plan according to your ASHRAE 188 Plan.
    • Notify all ASHRAE 188 Plan Team Members.

    Legionella

  • Detectable: Double the halogen dosage for the next feed.
  • 1‐9 cells/ml: Bring the free halogen up to 2 PPM for 8 hours.
  • 10‐99 cells/ml: Bring the free halogen up to 2 PPM for 24 hours.
  • 100‐999 cells/ml: Bring the free halogen up to 5 PPM for 24 hours.
  • 1000 cells/ml+:
    • Bring the free halogen up to 10 PPM for 24 hours.
    • Retest a 3‐7 days later.
