Engineering Services Applications

How to use this site

This site has maps specific to Engineering Services. Along the top menu, you will find a dropdown menu and two links. You can use the Applications dropdown to view maps that are available to staff in Engineering Services.

Note: the LiDAR Scans map will show what sections of the utility tunnels and what mechanical rooms have been digitally scanned. To view the 3D scans you will need a WebShare account. If you do not already have an account have your manager email Jay Dahlstrom to request access.

The UW Facilities Home link will take you to the UW Facilities Front Porch.

In the top right corner of every webpage, you will find a link labeled UW Facilities GIS Home this link will take you back to the dashboard homepage.

Questions or Comments?

If you have any questions or comments about the maps on this dashboard or if you are interested in starting a GIS project, please send an email to FS GIS and we will be happy to assist.
