Description The irrigation team within the grounds shop at the University of Washington uses this feature class to record the current status of each irrigation controller. That information is then communicated to other members of the grounds shop via an online map. This way gardeners can check in on the status of controllers in their maintenance area to understand what issues there are with these systems so they can plan their gardening work accordingly.
