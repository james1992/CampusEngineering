Description This feature class contains a copy of all building currently owned by the University of Washington on and around the Seattle Campus of the University of Washington. Following an earthquake Engineering Services is responsible for assessing these building to determine their structural integrity and if they can be reoccupied without repairs. This map collects the results of their inspections and makes the data available to UW Emergency Management and planners.
