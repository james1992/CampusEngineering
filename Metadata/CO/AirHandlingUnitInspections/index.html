Description This map identifies all of the buildings on the Seattle Campus of the University of Washington that have Air Handling Units (AHUs) that are inspected by the HVAC Shop in UW Facilities. As of May 2019, the map simply highlights for which buildings AHUs have been entered into the system. In the future it will operate as a status map and it will display the percentage of AHUs inspected in each building.
