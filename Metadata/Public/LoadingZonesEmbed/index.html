Description Feature class that identifies the location of loading zones, university vehicle parking and commercial loading zones on the Seattle Campus of the University of Washington. This information is used by UW personnel in locating a place to park on campus but it is primarily utilized by delivery companies that need to know where they can leave their vehicle on campus while making a delivery.
