Description Transportation Services at the University of Washington provides a service known as Dial-a-Ride. Dial-A-Ride is a fare-free shared-ride service that operates Monday to Friday from 8 a.m. until 7:25 p.m. excluding University holidays. Shuttles provide rides between 125 designated stop locations on the Seattle campus for UW staff, students and faculty. Visitors to the University on affiliated business may also qualify.
