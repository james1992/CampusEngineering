Title UW Campus Accessibility
Contributor The University of Washington Facilities Services, Engineering Services.
Coverage See Spatial Domain below.
This data covers the campus built environment as of December 2017 and contains information on accessible routes, building entrances and elevators.
Creator The University of Washington Facilities Services, Engineering Services.
Date 2017-9-29
Description This map represents an accessibility guide for persons with disabilities visiting the Seattle Campus of the University of Washington. Routes of varying degrees of accessibility are identified across campus that connect to accessible building entrances, inside of buildings accessible elevators are located on this map. The information in this map will be updated as the campus built environment changes due to construction or alteration projects. The routes, entrances and elevator locations should be used only as a point of reference and is not a gaurantee of accessibility.
